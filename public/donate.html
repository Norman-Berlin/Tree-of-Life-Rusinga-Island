About Tree of Life Children's Home

Tree of Life Children's Home was established in 2021 to provide shelter, care, and education to orphaned and vulnerable children in Rusinga Island. We believe every child deserves a safe, nurturing environment where they can grow and thrive.

Currently, we care for 50 children ranging from infants to teenagers. Our mission is to provide not just basic needs but also emotional support, education, and life skills to help these children become independent, responsible adults.
