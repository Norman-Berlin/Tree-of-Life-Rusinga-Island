About Tree of Life

Providing Hope on Rusinga Island

Founded in 2021, Tree of Life Children's Home is a safe haven for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children on Rusinga Island in Lake Victoria, Kenya. What began as a small initiative to help a few children in need has grown into a comprehensive care center that nurtures over 50 children at any given time.

Our home provides more than just shelter - we offer a loving family environment, quality education, healthcare, and life skills training to help each child reach their full potential. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive in a supportive community.

Rusinga Island, while beautiful, faces significant challenges including poverty, disease, and limited access to education. Tree of Life works to break the cycle of poverty by investing in the island's most precious resource - its children.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Core Values

Our Mission

To provide a loving, family-style home for orphaned and vulnerable children on Rusinga Island, offering comprehensive care that addresses their physical, emotional, educational, and spiritual needs while preparing them for independent, productive lives.

Our Vision

A world where every child on Rusinga Island grows up in a safe, nurturing environment with access to education, healthcare, and opportunities to break the cycle of poverty and become positive contributors to their community.
