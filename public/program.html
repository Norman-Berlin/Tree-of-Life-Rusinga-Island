Our Story

How we came to be a home for children in need

Welcome to Tree of Life

Founded in 2021, Tree of Life Children's Home is a safe haven for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children on Rusinga Island in Lake Victoria, Kenya. We provide a loving family environment, quality education, healthcare, and spiritual nourishment to help each child reach their full potential.

Our name comes from the biblical concept of the Tree of Life - representing growth, nourishment, and eternal hope. Just as a tree provides shelter and sustenance, we strive to provide these for the children in our care.

Our Mission

To provide a loving, family-style home for orphaned and vulnerable children, meeting their physical, emotional, educational, and spiritual needs.

Our Vision

To see each child grow into a responsible, empowered adult who contributes positively to their community.

Our Values

Love, Compassion, Integrity, Excellence, and Faith guide everything we do at Tree of Life.

A Day at Tree of Life

Our children wake at 6:30am for morning chores and breakfast. School-age children attend local schools while preschoolers learn at our on-site early childhood center. Evenings include homework help, playtime, Bible stories, and family-style dinners.

Weekends feature swimming in the lake, football matches, art projects, and community service activities.

Volunteer With Us

Join our mission on Rusinga Island

Make a Difference

We welcome volunteers from around the world to share their skills, time, and love with our children. Typical volunteer activities include:

  • Teaching and tutoring
  • Organizing recreational activities
  • Helping with maintenance projects
  • Medical professionals providing check-ups
  • Skills training for older children

Volunteers stay in our guest house and experience the beauty of Rusinga Island while making a lasting impact.
