Why Your Support Matters

This campaign isn't just about winning an election - it's about building a movement that puts people first. With your help, we can:

  • To fund school fees, supplies, and learning materials, empowering children with knowledge for a brighter future.
  • To cover medical expenses and ensure children receive proper care, vaccinations, and treatment when needed.
  • To maintain and improve living conditions, including repairs, utilities, and infrastructure upgrades.
  • To create a nurturing environment where children feel loved, valued, and safe, contributing to their mental and emotional health
