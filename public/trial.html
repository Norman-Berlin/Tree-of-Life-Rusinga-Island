Tree of Life Children's Home

Nestled on the serene shores of Rusinga Island, Tree of Life Children's Home is a sanctuary of hope, love, and opportunity for vulnerable children. Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and family-like environment where every child can grow, learn, and thrive.

Our Mission

At Tree of Life, we believe that every child deserves a chance to flourish—rooted in care, education, and community support. Through holistic care, quality education, and sustainable programs, we empower children to build brighter futures and become positive contributors to society.

Join us in planting seeds of change and helping children rise strong—because every child deserves a place to call home.
