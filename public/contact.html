Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tree of Life

How can I visit the children's home?

We welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact our volunteer coordinator at least one week in advance to schedule your visit. All visitors are required to complete a brief orientation before interacting with the children.

What are your visiting hours?

Our standard visiting hours are Monday to Friday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Weekend visits may be arranged for special circumstances. Please note that we don't allow visits during school hours (8:00 AM - 3:00 PM) or during the children's bedtime routine (after 8:00 PM).

How can I volunteer at Tree of Life?

We welcome volunteers for both short-term and long-term commitments. Please complete our volunteer application form and provide references. All volunteers must pass a background check. Typical volunteer roles include tutoring, mentoring, maintenance work, and special skills sharing.

What items can I donate to the children's home?

We always appreciate donations of school supplies, clothing (new or gently used), non-perishable food items, and hygiene products. Please contact us before bringing large items to ensure we have storage space. Financial donations are always welcome and allow us to purchase exactly what we need.

How do I sponsor a child?

Child sponsorship is $35 per month and provides food, clothing, education, and medical care for one child. Sponsors receive updates about their sponsored child and can exchange letters. Visit our Donate page or contact us directly to start the sponsorship process.

Tree of Life Children's Home provides love, care, and hope to vulnerable children on Rusinga Island, Kenya.
